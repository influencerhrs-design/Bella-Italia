# Bella Italia Restaurant Website

A professional restaurant website for Bella Italia, featuring authentic Italian cuisine.

## Features

- **Home Section**: Hero section with restaurant introduction and call-to-action buttons
- **About Section**: Information about the restaurant and its services
- **Menu Section**: Food items with prices and special discount offers
- **Team Section**: Meet the culinary team members
- **Testimonials**: Customer reviews and ratings
- **Contact Section**: Location map, phone number, email, and business hours
- **Social Media**: Links to Facebook, Instagram, and YouTube
